 高速数字通信的研究--同步交换芯片与长线传输

 
高速数字通信的研究--同步交换芯片与长线传输


随着计算机技术的发展，并行计算和并行处理已经成为当今计算机的热点。同时，并行计算机的性能提高越来越依赖相对较慢的通讯系统的功能和效率的提高。在硬件底层上，如何使数据传输变得更快，成为影响整个系统性能的关键因素。
随着机群系统的兴起，特别是工作站机群的兴起，机群节点间的分布比较分散，数据的异步传输方式使得长线问题在通讯系统效率上的影响越来越明显。而通讯系统的设计又直接关系到大型计算机系统的解题速度和解题规模。当前需要解决的问题，就是交换方式和长线传输。

本文讨论了机群机间通讯系统的硬件结构，分析了现有的通讯系统在有长线的情况下传输频率和带宽受到影响的主要因素，在交换方式方面，提出了把现有的异步交换改为同步交换的解决办法。参考国外先进的交换机的发展现状，分析它们的优缺点，并结合现有条件，讨论了交换机实现的技术策略，给出了一种同步交换芯片--- UX8的原型设计方案，包括调试方案和改进途径。

在针对长线传输问题方面，设计实现了一个系统原型MultiSlack，用它解决长线问题，目的是使得传输频率和线长无关。文章就该系统的模型做了理论分析，从系统的设计实现到测试都给出了详细说明，并就关键技术问题的原理和实现做了详细论述，罗列了部分测试结果，对其进行了分析。最后就问题的发展和方案的改进做了论述。 关键字：机群，交换，UX8，MultiSlack，同步逻辑，异步逻辑

ABSRACT

Luo Gang [major: computer architecture]
Directed by : Professor Zhu Mingfa

With the fast development of computer technology, parallel computing and parallel processing have become one of "hot" topics in current computer reserch field. In the same time, improving performance of parallel computers is becoming more dependent on the functions and efficiency of their slow-speed communication subsystems.Whether the system can quickly transfer data will be the key factor which affects the whole system performance.

As the clusters appears,especially the workstation clusters appear,the computing nodes become more disperse.The asynchronous transfer of data cause the high delay in long wire. The commuication subsystem can directly affect the speed and scale of sloving problem in large scale computer. Data switching method and long wire are two most urgent problems to solve .

This thesis discusses the hardware architecure of communicating system in clusters node,analyses key factors which influence frequency and bandwidth of current system with long wire .then the thesis discusses implement strategy of switching,provide an solution ---- UX8 crossbar switch,which replaces the current asynchronous switching method with synchronous one.

To solve the problem of long wire, an system ---- MultiSlack is given to make the transferring frequency independent on the length of wire.The design and implemention of multislack have been descriped very clearly in association chapters.And the key technology issues are talkd over in detail.At the end of the thesis, The development of the issue is also discussed.

Keyword: cluster, switching, UX8, MultiSlack, synchronized logic, asynchronized logic
