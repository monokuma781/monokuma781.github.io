 大规模工作流系统的分析设计

 
大规模工作流系统的分析设计


随着众多企业对复杂商业流程的协调和自动化需求的增加，数量和规模日益增长的工作流系统得到越来越广泛的应用。随着规模不断扩大，对系统的可用性、可扩展性、容错性提出了越来越高的要求。使得面向工作流的应用软件开发具有同普通MIS系统的开发所不具有的特殊性。

本文从现有工作流系统入手，首先分析了现有工作流系统中存在的缺陷，然后针对大规模工作流系统所要求的鲁棒性、可用性、可扩展性、对大规模并发用户和进程的支持，以及复杂流程定义的支持，系统地阐述了由此带来的对需求分析和系统设计所应该考虑到的特殊要求。对系统开发模型、系统需求分析和系统总体设计的关键问题进行了深入的探讨，并结合了所开发的商标注册与管理自动化系统二期工程(CTCSII)，论述了流程模型、长事务处理等关键技术问题，提出了完整的解决方案。

With more and more demand on the cooperation and the automation of complex business processes by many enterprises, the number and the scalability of workflow system which are used broadly have been increasing at a remarkable speed. As the scalability being greater and greater, there is great requirement on the system's reliability, scalability, fault-tolerance, which makes it be quite different from other Management Information System.

This paper starts with the analysis on the deficiency of current workflow systems, and then focuses on the properties of large scale workflow system on robustness, reliability, scalability, the ability of large scale parallel users and process and complex process definition. Discussing the analysis on requirement and design of large workflow system, the paper pays much attention on the key ideas of system development model, system requirement model and overall design. A full schema was introduced to demonstrate an example of CTCSII. in which some key problems were studied.
