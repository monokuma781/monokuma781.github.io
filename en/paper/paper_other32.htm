 机群作业管理系统研究

 
机群作业管理系统研究


计算机在50多年的发展中经历了五次重大的变革，大规模并行处理成为第五代计算机的最重要特征。机群系统以其卓越的性能价格比和良好的可扩展性等因素成为当今计算机体系结构与并行处理的发展方向，能够充分满足我们对计算机处理能力不断增长的需求，是当前的研究热点。

机群作业管理系统直接关系到机群性能的发挥和使用率的提高，是机群的一个重要组成部分。它能够充分利用机群的硬件软件资源及宝贵的CPU时间，有效地管理机群，合理调度作业，因此对机群作业管理系统的研究具有举足轻重的意义。

鉴于作业管理在机群系统中的重要地位，我们展开了对机群作业管理系统的研究。本文在总结前人工作的基础上，系统地研究了机群作业管理的关键问题，提出了机群作业调度的一种新的解决方案，最后就我们开发的基于曙光2000的作业管理系统进行了总结。主要工作包括：

1） 针对对机群作业管理系统的基本概念、组成、功能、特征等方面作了系统的概括。

2） 研究了几种MPP和机群系统上的作业管理系统，分析了它们的背景、基本结构、工作方式和功能特点，最后确立了作业管理系统的评判标准。

3） 从重要性的角度，设计与实现机群作业管理系统的关键问题可分为六个方面：可扩展性、负载平衡、检查点切取、进程迁移、作业调度及其它（资源管理、容错性、多用户特性等）。这几个方面既包括机群作业管理系统的重点，也包括难点。本文就当前它们的研究现状和解决方法进行了论述。

4） 作业调度是体现公平对待用户作业，提高系统响应时间，进而提高系统性能的关键因素。本文提出并设计了基于串行作业优先（Sequential-First）公平作业调度策略。

5） 为了进一步提高系统吞吐率，本文提出了受限负载平衡（LLBA）结点分配策略。模拟结果证实了LLBA相对于传统的自适应结点分配策略能够进一步有助于系统吞吐率的提高。

6） 通过对作业管理系统及其关键问题的研究，我们设计并实现了曙光2000作业管理系统joss。本文对它的设计思路、拓扑结构、运行过程、关键问题处理和功能特征等方面进行了介绍。

关键词 机群系统，机群作业管理，作业调度，结点分配，负载平衡，检查点切取，进程迁移，资源管理，吞吐率，平均响应时间

Computer development has undergone five revolutions from 1945, when the modern computer era, until now. Massive Parallel Processing is the most important character of the fifth generation of computers. Because of its outstanding price/performance radio and scalability, cluster system is becoming the trend of Computer Architecture and Parallel Processing. It is completely able to satisfy our requirement that keeps increasing to computer performance unceasingly. Clusters of workstations are increasingly viewed as a cost-efficient alternative to parallel supercomputer. It is the hotpot of current research.

Job Management System (JMS) is critical to give full play to the performance of cluster and improve the rate of its utilization. It can make reasonable use of the system resources and expensive CPU time, effectively manage cluster system, and equitably schedule jobs. Thus, many researchers show solicitude for Job Management for Cluster.

This thesis investigates the essential issues of job management for cluster, proposes one novel algorithm for job scheduling to increase the throughput of a cluster, and introduces the job management system JOSS we developed for Dawning 2000, which is cluster-based computer. It includes the following parts:

1. The basic concepts, components, functions and characters of job management system for cluster are presented.

2. Five representatives of JMS for MPPs and clusters, such as Loadleveler, LSF, are introduced. Their basic structures and characteristics are analyzed. In addition, the evaluation criterion of JMS is presented.

3. The essential issues of researching and designing JMS for cluster include six respects: scalability, load balance, checkpointing, process migrant, job scheduling, and others (resource management, multi-user process, and so on). The thesis shows the current situation of their research.

4. Job scheduling is one of the most factors to run users' jobs, which belong to difficult users fairly, reduce the response time of cluster, and improve the performance of cluster. The Sequential-First job-scheduling algorithm is proposed in the thesis.

5. In order to make further improvement on the throughput of cluster system, we present a new adaptive allocation algorithm: Limited Load Balancing Allocation (LLBA) in Chapter 6. Through simulation experiment, we compare LLBA with the traditional load balancing allocation and find that significant performance can be obtained by LLBA.

6. Based on our research on JMS of Cluster, we design and implement JOSS, the job management system for Dawning2000. Chapter 7 introduces its thoughts, topologic architecture, and the solutions of some critical issues and function characters of JOSS.

KEYWORDS Cluster, cluster job management system, job scheduling, node location, load balance, checkpointing, process migrating, resource management, throughput, average response time
