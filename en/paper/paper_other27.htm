 机群并行计算环境的研究、设计和实现

 
机群并行计算环境的研究、设计和实现


机群就是由一组计算机系统（结点）通过高性能网络或局部网互联而形成的具有单一系统映象的高可用、高性能、高可扩展性的计算机集群系统。机群的发展与机群并行计算环境的发展是分不开的。机群的并行计算环境包括资源管理，通信子模块以及协同操作子模块。本文结合了作者在DPVM(基于曙光2000的PVM)的开发实践，对机群并行计算环境进行了研究。本文首先对机群，机群并行计算环境进行了广泛和深入的研究。然后分析了曙光2000为并行计算环境提供的一般异构机群不具有的种种优势，在此基础上设计和实现了曙光2000DPVM。本文详细地介绍了DPVM的原理、技术以及实现的策略和方法，并对它的性能进行了分析。

本文介绍的重点之一是基于曙光2000同构机群的并行计算环境PVM的设计和实现。这个并行计算环境基于Myrinet高速网络，曙光2000的精简通信协议BCL以及曙光机群的资源管理子系统RMS。Myrinet和BCL为DPVM提供了强大的通信能力，RMS有力的保证了DPVM在资源管理，协同操作等方面与标准PVM的兼容性。测试结果证明，DPVM不仅与标准PVM在语义上完全兼容，并且能够为用户提供远比基于TCP/IP的标准PVM强大的性能。

关键字：机群，并行计算环境，PVM，MPI，机群通信，，TCP/IP。

the Research, Design and Implementation on Cluster-based
parallel Computing Environment

Zhao Peng (Computer Theory)
Directed by: Xu Zhiwei

A cluster is a group of high-performance computers connected by a high-performance interconnection network or a high-speed LAN to achieve high availability, high performance and high scalability with a single system image. The development of clusters is closely related to their parallel computing environments(PCE). PCE includes resource management module, communication module and collective operation module, etc.
This paper is based on the development practices on the Dawning 2000 PVM (DPVM) project..

In this paper, we first give an overview of clusters in the following aspects: the development trend, the architecture, and the main features. Then we discuss DPVM's multitudinous advantages provided by Dawning 2000, and explore the details of the design and implementation of DPVM. The performance evaluation is also provided.

The emphasis of this paper is the design and the implementation of the PCE in Dawning homogeneous cluster. This PCE is supported by Myrinet high-speed network, BCL(a simplified communication protocol designed by Dawning 2000 project) and Resource Management System(RMS). Myrinet and BCL provide powerful communication capability for DPVM. Thanks to RMS, DPVM is able to be fully compatible with standard PVM in resource management and collective operation. After careful performance evaluation, we can draw the conclusion that DPVM is not only semantically compatible with standard PVM, but much more powerful than TCP/IP based PVM.

Keywords: clusters, parallel computing environment, PVM, MPI, cluster communication, TCP/IP
