安全协议的验证逻辑


一个安全协议提出后，必须从各方面对其安全性进行验证。在众多的验证方法中，逻辑手段凭借其严密、简洁的特点，不仅在学术界占据了重要地位，而且在工业和金融行业中也产生了重大影响。在这方面，已经有人做了大量的研究工作，在提出的众多的用于分析验证安全协议的逻辑框架中，NDL逻辑充分体现了通信安全问题中的“动态性”和“非单调性”，引入了动态逻辑的机制，通过“失败即否定”等原则作为演绎推理的补充，实现了一种把普通的一阶逻辑和动态逻辑、非单调逻辑综合起来的逻辑框架。

本文对NDL逻辑框架进行了扩展。并在此基础上对Kerberos协议和SET协议的安全性问题进行了深入研究，我们主要着重于在建立的安全框架中实现“不可抵赖”和“不可追踪”两种特性。并希望结合NDL的“失败即否定”的特点，实现用Prolog程序实现安全协议验证系统。

在此基础上，我们简单介绍了Kerberos协议的整个流程，并针对Kerberos协议，增加新的谓词、公理。并用扩展后的逻辑框架对Kerberos协议的整个流程进行逻辑验证。其后，我们结合知道逻辑中的“传令兵问题”，给出了传令兵问题的逻辑描述，并证明了Kerberos的安全性是达到了传令兵问题中要求的强度的，且给出了“传令兵问题”的逻辑描述。

从理论上说，“传令兵问题”的安全性实际上是一个无限的过程，但我们可以证明，存在一个正整数N，只要在发送了N个消息之后，能够使CO_believe()(A，B，X)和Co_believe1(A，B，X)成立，那么在此之后，一定可以达到传令兵问题所要求的安全强度。这样，便将-个无限的问题转换为一个有限的问题。

此后，在验证了SET的“不可追踪性”之后，针对文[32]中提出的SET中可能存在的“k out of n”问题，充分考虑了实际的金融业务中可能会发生的情况，对SET协议的安全性问题进行了研究，SET实际上是无法保证“不可抵赖性”的，我们通过研究给出了解决方案。在原有设备的基础上，增加访问控制权限来实现的和这是管理Proxy，就可以基本上保证“不可抵赖性”的实现。

最后想指出的是，我们通过对验证逻辑在信息安全方面的一些尝试和研究，说明了形式化逻辑方法确实可以在这个领域上发挥重要作用。随着电子商务的日益普及，它还有很多可以值得我们进行研究的地方，比如：一些具体的电子商务，象股票，期货交易的验证系统；在复杂的商业背景下，如何实现两种安全机制等，这些都是我们今后的主要研究方向。

After a secure protocol is bought forward, the security must be verified on its various aspects as possible as we can. In so many verifying methods, the logical ways play an important role not only on academia but also on industry and finance for its strictness and concision. There are many researchers who make a lot of researches about that, but in such a lot of existed logical framework used to verify secure protocol, only NDL embody the "Dynamic" and "Non-monotonic" adequately. It introduces the Dynamic logic mechanism, and uses the principle "Failing approach Negation" to reinforce the deduction reasoning. It realizes a kind of logic framework that integrates the Predicate logic, Dynamic logic and Non-monotonic logic.

This paper extend NDL framework, and then do some further research on the security of Kerberos and SET. We mostly want to realize the "Non-deniability" and "Non-Traceability" Also, combined with the principle "Failing approach Negation" , we want to use the Prolog program to simulate the verification of secure protocols .

Based on this, we simply introduce the flow of Kerberos. Then we extend the NDL according to the requirement of Kerberos, and use the extended NDL to verify Kerberos protocol. In addition, we introduce the "Batman problem" in Knowledge Logic, give a logic description of it and then verify the security of Kerberos can be the same as the required security of the "Batman problem".

Theoretically speaking, the security of batman problem is a infinite procedure. But we can prove that there exists a integer N, after sending N messages, if it can satisfy C0_believe0(A,B,X) and Co_believe1(A,B,X), then it sure can reach the security that the batman problem required. Thus, we turn an infinite problem to a definite one.
After verifying the "Non-traceability" character of SET, according to the "k out of n" problem in reference [32], we consider adequately the secure problem of SET in practical business. In fact, SET can't assure the "Non-deniability ". Through the research, we give out a solution to that problem. It is based on the original equipment, and is realized by adding the visited instruction that only known to the unique person and a Proxy to storage the certificate of each participator. They can basically assure the realization of "Non-deniability".

Finally, through the attempt and research on verification logic in the field of information security, we point that formal logic method is sure playing a main role in it. As the popularity of electronic Commerce, there will have many problem that worth to research as stock, futures's transaction systems. So the main research direction of us is that how to assure the "non-deniability" and "non-traceability" in such complex environment.
