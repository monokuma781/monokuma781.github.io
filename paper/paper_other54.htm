 视频点播服务器设计关键技术研究

 
视频点播服务器设计关键技术研究

摘要

多媒体交互是用户交流信息方式的必然进化。多媒体服务的应用领域非常广泛，其中视频点播(Video-on-Demand, VoD)应用最为广泛。视频点播技术是随着计算机技术和网络通讯技术的发展，综合了计算机技术、通讯技术、电视技术而迅速新兴的一门综合性技术。它利用了网络和视频技术的优势，彻底改变了过去收看节目的被动方式，实现了节目的按需收看和任意播放，集动态影视图像、静态图片、声音、文字等信息为一体，是为用户提供实时、交互、按需点播的服务的系统。

视频服务器是视频服务系统功能实现和性能质量保证的核心，因此也成为整个视频服务系统研究的重点。视频服务不仅要求保证端到端的播放质量，同时也要考察系统的整体性能。整体性能常用最大并发用户数这一参数来评价。为使这一指标尽可能高，需要从很多方面解决视频服务器设计的关键问题。确定哪些是视频服务器设计中的提高性能的关键问题，并且对这些关键问题进行研究，进而提出自己的改进方法是本论文的主要内容。其关键问题主要集中在以下几个方面：视频流编码压缩及传输技术、存储和I/O调度策略、视频流调度技术、流量控制与差错控制、接入控制、IP网与Cable网实现、视频服务器体系结构等。作者将对这些关键问题进行深入的研究探讨和分析，并做出评价和总结。

本论文在分析这些关键问题的基础上，将重点放在其中的节目流调度问题和传输流复用问题上。作者研究了对视频服务整体性能有着重大提升作用的视频流调度策略，在应用多播技术的前提下，比较了各种现有解决方案的优劣，最终提出一种支持VCR(录像机)操作的称之为可重组Patching方法的视频流调度方案并对其性能详细评价。本文的另一个重点是研究了CATV网络环境下MPEG-2传输流的复用算法，该算法用于将多个节目复用在一个传输流中传输，分析已有算法的基础上，提出了一种称之为Best-Effort的算法并分析和评价了其性能。最后对所有工作作出了结论，并对下一步工作提出了展望。

关键词：视频点播 视频服务器 流媒体 流调度 传输流复用


Research on Key Issues of Design of Video-on-Demand Server

Abstract

Interacting by multimedia is consequentially evolution of people interaction manner. Application of Multimedia service is very broad, and Video-on-Demand of which is the most frequently used one. Video-on-Demand technology was developed along with development of computer technology and network communication, which is synthesis of computer 、communication and television technology. It takes most advantage of network and video technology, which totally modifies the passive style of receive TV program and realizes video received on demand and play on will, which is synthesis of image、static image、sound、words and serves people with real-time、interactive and on demand service.

Video server is core of implement function of video service system and guarantee of performance and quality, so it becomes the core of whole video system. Video server requires not only guaranteeing quality of end-to-end service, also taking integral system performance into account. The integral performance is often evaluated by parameter Max Parallel User Quantity. In order to maximize the MPUQ, many key issues of design need to be settled. Main content of this paper contains analyzing clear what are the key issues of design video server to improve system performance, deep research into these key issues and bringing forward new resolve or development method. The key issues focus on following aspects: video coding and decoding technology, storage and I/O scheduling policy, video stream scheduling technology, flow control and error control, admission control, IP network and CATV network implement, architecture of video system. Author researchs deeply into these key issues and bring forward new resolve or development method.

On the basis of analyzing these key issues, author layes a strong emphasis on the study of video stream scheduling policy and multiplex algorithm of Transport Stream. Author studyes the video stream scheduling policy, which contributes greatly to system integral performance. On the basis of application of multicast technology and analyzing of existing evolutions, author put forward a new policy, called Re-groupable Patching(RGP) scheme, which support VCR function and analyzes its performance. Author also researches multiplex algorithm of MPEG-2 Transport Stream under CATV network environment, which used to multiplex TS stream of several programs into one TS stream, on the basis of analyzing existing algorithm, author presents a new algorithm called Best-Effort multiplex algorithm then analyzes and evaluates it. At last author makes a conclusion, which summarizes the whole work of the paper and refer to the future work of the VoD system.

Key Words：Video-on-Demand, Video Server, Stream Media, Stream Scheduling, TS Multiplexing
