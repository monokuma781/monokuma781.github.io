对曙光并行高可用服务器聚集监控台的研究


自九十年代中期以来，国外的企业信息系统出现了一种以服务器聚集为特征的，向集中式系统回归的潮流，最近在IT领域内对服务器聚集系统的研究和开发方兴未艾。服务器聚集产品及其应用程序被广泛应用于商务，企业和研究机构。服务器聚集以其高可用性和良好的可扩展性日益受到业界的关注。

本文从阐述服务器聚集技术的产生、发展、优点和现状出发，介绍了服务器聚集的技术问题。接着文章阐述了高可用性机群系统中的关键技术。文章重点结合自己的开发实践和体会详细论述了曙光并行高可用服务器聚集监控台的设计和实现。

曙光并行高可用服务器聚集产品对主控界面进行了特别的研究和设计，使得整个系统的入口具备简洁明了、易于使用、功能齐全、扩展容易、帮助友好等特色。在系统的设计和实现过程中，也始终考虑减轻系统管理员的任务，将对整个系统的管理图形化、界面化、规范化，尽量使用直观简明的方式让系统管理员及时得到整个系统的各种资源和配置的信息，既减轻了管理员的负担，又减少了由于误操作导致的失误。

本文还对服务器聚集技术未来的发展，高可用性技术和机群技术的结合与发展，以及曙光并行高可用服务器聚集监控台未来的改进谈了一些看法，可以作为进一步研究的参考。

关键词 服务器聚集 高可用性 机群系统 监控台


In the mid 1990's, there comes a trend in the enterprise information system to go back to the centralized system featured with Server Consolidation. Nowadays research and development of Server Consolidation in the IT field is on the ascendant. Server Consolidation products and its applications are widely used in business，enterprise and institutes. The Server Consolidation is catching more and more eyes in the computer community because of its high availability and scalability.

In this article，the generation, development，and technical issue of Server Consolidation are presented first. Then introduce the key technical issues of high availability Cluster system. According to the author's own experience, the design and implementation of Dawning Parallel High Availability Serve Cluster Administration developed with Java are introduced in detail.

In order to make the entrance of the whole system terse, usable, functional, scalable, user-friendly, the product of Dawning Parallel High Availability Serve Cluster has a special research and design on the main control interface. Considering the task of the system administrator, we make the administration of whole system graphical, interfacial, normative during the design and implementation. The system administrator can get the various resource and configuration information of the whole system by intuitionistic means so as to alleviate the load of the administrator and lessen the misplay.

The future of the Server Consolidation technology, the combination and development of high availability and Scalable Cluster, and further work of Dawning Parallel High Availability Serve Cluster Administration are also involved which can act as a reference for further research.

KeyWords: Server Consolidation, High Available , Cluster System, Administration
