 对高可用系统的主动监控管理的研究

 
对高可用系统的主动监控管理的研究

近年来，联机事务处理系统在商业领域的应用日益广泛，市场需求持续增长。这类系统对服务连续性、数据完整性、短故障时间、长时间运行的技术要求，使高可用系统得到了广泛的重视。目前，发达国家都在积极研制和开发高可用系统。为了填补我国在高可用系统技术上的空白，力争在这一新的领域达到国际先进水平，国家智能机研究中心设立了高可用系统的研究和开发的课题。

本文首先介绍了高可用系统的基本概念及其可用度的马尔可夫模型，包括高可用（HA）系统的基本概念，并提出了一种对高可用系统的主动监控管理的方案，即HA ADM。本文阐述了HA ADM系统的总体设计，软硬件结构和工作机制。HA ADM系统的核心是与HA核心间主动的通讯方式。文中对这项技术的概念和基本思想作了详细的介绍。文中还主要阐述了在UNIX AIX平台和Windows NT平台上完成的HA ADM的实现。最后，对存在的问题和进一步的工作还作了简单的阐述。

Recently, on-line transaction processing(OLTP) applications are widely used in the commercial field. The market of OLTP applications is growing continuously. Highly available systems, which provided service continuity, data integrity, shortly down time and long mission times, are needed for OLTP applications. The developed countries are doing more and more research and development in highly available systems. For the purpose of filling the blank in highly available systems field and catching up with the international advanced technique level, the National Center of Intelligent Computing systems(NCIC) set up a project aimed at researching and developing highly available systems.

In this thesis, we first introduce the fundamental concepts of and Markov availability model of highly available systems, including the basic concepts of High Availability (HA). Then give a new solution called active administration on high Availability system (HA ADM). We also illustrate the designate, architecture and working mechanism of the HA ADM. The core technique of the HA ADM is the active communication way between the Core of HA system. We first introduce the concepts and main ideas of active monitor Implementations of HA ADM on UNIX AIX(IBM) platform and Windows NT(Microsoft) are also given. Finally we discuss the limitations of the implementation and the future works.
